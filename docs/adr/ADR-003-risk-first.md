# ADR-003

## Title

Risk Management has higher priority than Trading Signals.

---

## Status

Accepted

---

## Context

High-confidence trading signals should never bypass risk controls.

---

## Decision

Every trading decision must pass through Risk & Money Management before execution.

Risk Management has authority to reject any trade regardless of signal confidence.

---

## Consequences

Pros

- Capital protection.
- Consistent behavior.
- Professional trading workflow.

Cons

- Some profitable trades may be rejected.
