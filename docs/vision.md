# KEMET

> Finance Intelligence Platform

---

# Vision

KEMET is an open-source Finance Intelligence Platform designed to help traders make transparent, explainable, and data-driven decisions through a collaborative Multi-Agent AI architecture.

Rather than relying on a single trading strategy or a single AI model, KEMET coordinates multiple specialized AI agents. Each agent focuses on one responsibility, including financial news analysis, macroeconomic events, technical indicators, market regime detection, market sentiment, quantitative analysis, and risk management.

All agents report their findings to a central AI Orchestrator responsible for evaluating evidence, resolving conflicting opinions, assigning confidence scores, and producing the most reliable trading decision.

KEMET follows a three-layer intelligent architecture:

- **Intelligence Layer** — Specialized AI Agents responsible for collecting information, analyzing markets, and generating knowledge.

- **Decision Layer** — AI Orchestrator, Decision Engine, Risk & Money Management, Position Management, and Execution Strategy responsible for transforming knowledge into safe, explainable trading decisions.

- **Continuous Learning Layer** — Responsible for collecting historical experience, evaluating performance, improving agent quality, optimizing prompts, updating machine learning models, and continuously increasing the intelligence of the entire platform.

Unlike traditional trading systems, KEMET is designed to evolve continuously.

Every prediction, every executed trade, every market movement, every success, and every failure becomes a learning opportunity.

Knowledge generated by every agent is stored, evaluated, and transformed into measurable improvements through a controlled learning pipeline.

The objective is not to improve individual agents only, but to continuously improve the intelligence of the entire platform.

The first production module focuses on XAUUSD (Gold), while the architecture is intentionally modular to support Forex, Indices, Stocks, Cryptocurrencies, Commodities, and other financial markets without redesigning the platform.

---

# Philosophy

KEMET is not designed to replace traders.

It is designed to augment human decision-making by combining multiple independent AI agents, transparent reasoning, disciplined risk management, and continuous learning.

Every recommendation generated by KEMET must be explainable, measurable, reproducible, and supported by evidence.

KEMET never guarantees profits.

Its mission is to maximize decision quality while minimizing unnecessary risk.
