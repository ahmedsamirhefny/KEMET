# Glossary

## Agent

A specialized software component responsible for a single business capability.

---

## AI Orchestrator

Coordinates all agents and produces the final decision.

---

## Decision Engine

Transforms knowledge into actionable trading decisions.

---

## Learning Layer

Evaluates historical performance and continuously improves the platform.

---

## Position Management

Responsible for managing open positions after execution.

---

## Execution Strategy

Determines how trading decisions are delivered to the user or broker.

---

## Knowledge Base

Stores historical knowledge collected from all agents.
